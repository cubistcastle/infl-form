Service members, veterans, and their families

Please select the option below that best describes you in order for Columbia College Chicago to provide service members, veterans, and their families with proper information regarding financial aid, veteran and service member support services, as well as tools for educational planning.

  • Veteran

    Any person who served on active duty in the U.S. Armed Forces, discharged or released under conditions other than dishonorable or any individual who served in the reserves or National Guard and discharged under honorable circumstances.

  • Reservists and National Guard

    Any person currently serving in the U.S. Armed Forces, not on active duty but may be called in an emergency.

  • Disabled Veteran

    Determined to be disabled by VA as result of service.

  • Dependent of Veterans, Active Duty, Reservists, National Guard, or Disabled Veteran individuals

    A child or spouse of a veteran, active duty, reservist or National Guard, or disabled veteran service member.

  • Dependent of MIA, KIA, or POW Service Member

    Child or spouse of a deceased veteran, active duty, reservist or National Guard, or disabled veteran who died of service connected illness or injury from MIA, KIA or POW.

  • None of the above

    None of these describe me.

Thank you.

It’s very important to us that we provide service members, veterans, and their families with proper information regarding financial aid, veteran and service member support services, as well as tools for educational planning.
